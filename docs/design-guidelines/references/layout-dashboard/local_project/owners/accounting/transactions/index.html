Accounting FAQs

What does TurboTenant Accounting do?
TurboTenant Accounting is software specifically for rental property owners and real estate investors. Our goal is to make investment property bookkeeping easier, more efficient, and less stressful.
What is REI Hub?
REI Hub is the company that originally created the best real estate specific bookkeeping solution on the market. Previously just a partnership, they are now owned by TurboTenant. Today the same REI Hub software and team powers TurboTenant Accounting.
How does this differ from other accounting software?
Traditional accounting software like Quickbooks is built for generic small businesses and their accountants. They support a wide range of industries and customer types. This results in cluttered software that is hard to navigate and not user friendly. They are NOT specifically built for rental property bookkeeping. On the other hand, TurboTenant Accounting is exclusively focused on the unique needs of the investment property owner. This focus allows us to concentrate on providing the best and easiest to use accounting software for real estate investors and rental property owners like you.
How does it integrate with the rest of TurboTenant?
The Accounting tool will automatically link to your TurboTenant account. Payments and expenses recorded in TurboTenant will be automatically reflected in your books and categorized by property, unit, or portfolio. No need to re-enter any details.
How do you integrate with my bank accounts?
TurboTenant Accounting uses secure Data Providers (Plaid and Yodlee) to access a read-only transaction feed from your checking, savings, and credit card accounts. They take your privacy and security very seriously. So TurboTenant Accounting never has access to your sensitive log-in credentials and information. More information about our Data Providers Plaid and Yodlee is available on their websites.
Do I need to use Rent Payments to use Accounting?
No. You can use TurboTenant Accounting without using Rent Payments in TurboTenant. Your properties and expenses will still be synced.
What reports are included?

Complete Financial Reporting

  • Profit and Loss and Statement of Cash Flows - available for unit, property, or portfolio
  • Balance Sheet
  • Schedule E for each rental, ready to go for you or your accountant at tax time.
  • Fixed Asset Tracking so you can deduct depreciation and account for capital expenses.

Other Reports Include:

  • Net Operating Income
  • Net Income by Rental
  • Rent Roll
  • Cash on Cash
  • Cap Rate
  • Operating Expense Ratio
  • Tax Packet for you or your tax preparer to help you maximize deductions
  • Expenses by Vendor
  • Security Deposits Held
  • Trial Balance
Is customer support available for Accounting help?
Yes, we have a specific customer support team trained in real estate bookkeeping from the REI Hub team. Email support is available at info@reihub.net and phone support is available during business hours (9am-5pm EST) at (888) 939-6865.
How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on the number of units you've added. You can choose between monthly or annual billing:

Monthly Pricing:

  • $10/mo for 1-2 units
  • $25/mo for 3-10 units
  • $45/mo for 11-20 units
  • $80/mo for 21+ units

Annual Pricing (20% discount):

  • $96/year for 12 units
  • $240/year for 310 units
  • $432/year for 1120 units
  • $768/year for 21+ units

Room rentals are not considered units and therefore are not counted towards your TurboTenant Accounting subscription or listed as separately trackable items. Transaction data from your rooms will be shown at the property or unit to which the rooms belong.

Are you able to handle accounting for short-term rentals?
Yes, TurboTenant Accounting can handle accounting for short-term rentals. Even if youre not using TurboTenant to manage these properties, you should add your short-term properties to TurboTenant to ensure they show up in TurboTenant Accounting.
